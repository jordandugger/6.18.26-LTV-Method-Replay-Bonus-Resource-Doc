How to Save & Restore Your Data
This tool runs locally in your browser — no account needed. Use JSON export/import to save your numbers and reload them anytime, or drop your data into a Claude conversation to get AI analysis.
Step 1 — Saving Your Data
1

Click "Export Data" in the top-right header

This downloads a file called ifca-tracker-[year].json to your Downloads folder. It contains all numbers you've entered across every tab — Goal Math, Constraint Finder, and Monthly Tracker.

2

Store it somewhere you'll remember

Save it to Google Drive, Dropbox, or a folder called "IFCA Business Tracking." The file name includes the year so you can keep separate files per year.

Step 2 — Restoring Your Data
1

Click "Import JSON" in the top-right header

Select your saved .json file. All your numbers will restore instantly across every tab. Works even after closing and reopening your browser.

Step 3 — Getting AI Analysis from Claude
1

Export your JSON file

Download your data using the "Export Data" button after you've entered your monthly numbers.

2

Go to claude.ai and start a new conversation

Drag and drop (or upload via the paperclip) your JSON file into the Claude chat window.

3

Paste this prompt:

You are an IFCA business coach. I've uploaded my Data Constraints Tracker JSON file containing my monthly KPI data. Please analyze my numbers across the 5 business levers (Traffic, Pipeline, Sales Conversion, Back-End, Financials) and tell me: 1. What is my #1 constraint right now based on my data? 2. Which metrics are below IFCA benchmarks and by how much? 3. What is the math showing about my path to [YOUR GOAL $X/month]? 4. What are the 3 highest-leverage actions I should take in the next 30 days? Here are the IFCA benchmarks for context: - CPL target: <$10 | Show Rate: >70% | Close Rate: 30-40% - AOV: $3K-$6K | LTV: $6K-$12K+ | Net Margin: >50% - FE→BE conversion: >50% | Contracted ROAS: >5x | Collected ROAS: >2x
4

Claude will diagnose your constraint and give you a prioritized action plan

You can then ask follow-up questions: "What should my ad creative focus on?", "How do I fix my show rate?", "What does a backend offer look like for my niche?" — Claude will give specific recommendations based on your actual numbers.

Pro Tip — Monthly Tracking Rhythm
Every 1st of the month: Open this tool, import your previous JSON, fill in last month's numbers across all 5 sections, export the updated JSON and save it. Takes about 5 minutes. After 90 days, you'll have a clear picture of your quarter — which levers moved, which didn't, and exactly where to focus next.

Quarterly: Drop the JSON into Claude with the analysis prompt above. Print the constraint diagnosis. This is the exact data your IFCA success coach will want to review on your calls.
