P3 · Watch Item
Fear of Group Calls — Scale Ceiling Mindset Block
Katie explicitly mentioned fear of implementing weekly group calls as a constraint. In a 6-month program with growing client volume, individual 1:1 calls become a time bottleneck that caps revenue regardless of lead flow. If she cannot transition to any group delivery model, her maximum monthly revenue is capped by the number of 1:1 hours she can physically provide.
The Reframe: "Group coaching" and "group Q&A" are different things. The prescription is a hybrid model: individual onboarding call (1:1, high-touch, relationship-building) + weekly group Q&A session (30-45 min, all clients together, question-driven, not presentation-driven). The group Q&A is not coaching — it is a community call. This format is significantly less emotionally demanding and can be positioned to clients as an added bonus rather than a replacement for 1:1 access.
