Henry's Instagram bio and content strategy funnels followers toward a $199 Playbook app product as the primary conversion action. His 1:1 coaching — which is almost certainly worth $2,000-3,500 and is the highest-margin, highest-impact product he has — is buried behind a Linktree link with no dedicated landing page, no testimonials, and no content driving toward it. The $199 product requires 75 monthly buyers to generate $15K. The coaching program requires 5.
The Fix: Build a dedicated landing page for "The Rehab Meets Performance Protocol" — 12-16 week program, priced $2,500-3,500, with clinical rehab + strength programming phases and specific outcome milestones. Update Instagram bio to point to this landing page as the primary CTA. Keep Playbook as a secondary/proof link. The Playbook buyers become the warm ascension audience for the coaching program.
P1 · Primary Constraint
No DM Funnel — No Path From 12.2K Followers to Coaching
Henry's organic content generates engagement, saves, and follows — but there is no mechanism to convert that passive engagement into qualified sales conversations. No "Comment REHAB" CTA, no DM automation, no free guide opt-in, no direct path from "I found this content helpful" to "I want to book a coaching call." Every follower who resonates with his content but doesn't get an active CTA is a permanently missed lead.
The Fix: Add "Comment REHAB for my free pain-free lifting guide" to every content post immediately — new and retroactive. Set up ManyChat: auto-DM with free guide PDF + follow-up sequence with consultation booking link. This single change turns Henry's already-excellent content into an active lead generation machine. Estimated timeline to first result: 7-14 days from implementation.
P2 · Meaningful Gap
Zero Paid Ads — Ad-Ready Hooks Not Being Deployed at Scale
Henry has at least 5 organically-proven hooks that meet IFCA ad creative standards — specific pain callouts, contrarian reveals, outcome-led education. These hooks are not being amplified with any paid budget. The result is that his reach is capped at organic distribution and his conversion volume is limited to whoever happens to find his content. Paid amplification of existing creative is the fastest revenue unlock at this stage.
The Fix: Take the top 3 performing organic hooks (Stop stretching hamstrings; Knee pain squat; Traditional PT villain) and run them as paid ads at $30/day targeting: powerlifters, CrossFitters, recreational athletes with injury interests. Start with retargeting (engagers, website visitors). Expect CPL under $10 with these specific hooks. Scale cold traffic after retargeting baseline is established.
P3 · Watch Item
No Ascension Architecture — Playbook Buyers Are Stranded
Every person who buys the $199 Playbook and gets results is a qualified warm lead for 1:1 coaching. Without an automated email sequence that positions coaching as the natural next step, these buyers either stop after the program (lost LTV) or ascend randomly without any system capturing them. This is $2,000-3,000 per buyer sitting on the table.

Here's a tip for week 2"; (2) Day 21 — "Most people at this stage hit [specific plateau] — here's what 1:1 coaching addresses that the program can't"; (3) Day 42 — "You've finished the program. Here is how 1:1 coaching takes you from where you are now to your next performance goal — free consultation this week."
