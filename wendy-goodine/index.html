Strategic Diagnosis

Wendy’s situation is the most straightforward in this cohort: she already has everything she needs, it just isn’t visible yet. The copy works. The offers exist. The proof is extraordinary. The only missing ingredient is distribution. One honest talking-head reel about losing 95 pounds and keeping it off for 25 years — filmed on a phone, no production value required — has the potential to generate more qualified leads than anything else she could do in the next 90 days. Start there.

Section 04
Constraint Diagnosis
P1 — Critical / Growth Blocker
Zero Digital Reach — Referral-Only Growth Has a Hard Ceiling
10 clients from pure referrals is impressive proof that the offer works — but referral-only acquisition caps at the size of the existing network, and that ceiling is close. Without organic content building a broader audience and without paid ads scaling it, growth will plateau and stall. The extraordinary personal story, the confirmed website copy, and the existing lead magnet are all sitting idle without traffic to amplify them.
Fix: Start publishing 3–5 reels per week immediately. Priority: the 95-lb personal story reel first, then the website hook deployed as talking-head content, then villain frame content. Every post ends with: “Comment REFRAME for my free post-menopause nutrition guide.” Set a 30-day content sprint before evaluating paid ads.
P2 — Meaningful Gap
No Visible High-Ticket Pricing — Invisible Offer Value
Neither named offer (“3-Week Nutrition Makeover” or “The Better Than Ever Collective”) has visible pricing online. When a potential client cannot quickly understand what the investment is, they disengage before inquiring. More importantly, without a defined high-ticket offer at $2,500–$3,500, Wendy is likely undercharging for the transformation she delivers, which limits revenue per client and makes the math to six figures much harder.
Fix: Define the Better Than Ever Protocol as the primary high-ticket offer at $2,500–$3,500 for a 4–6 month engagement. Build a simple landing page with the offer, the 25-year proof story, and one CTA (book a free call). The Collective becomes the lower-ticket backend ($97–$147/mo) for clients who complete the protocol.
P2 — Meaningful Gap
95-lb Story Not Deployed as Content — Most Valuable Asset Sitting Idle
The 95-lb personal story maintained for 25 years is the single most powerful credibility asset Wendy has. It directly answers the target avatar’s #1 fear (“I’ll just gain it back”) and is completely unique in the post-menopause coaching space. It has never been filmed. It has never been used as a reel, an ad, or even a consistent bio claim. Until it is, the most compelling piece of social proof in Wendy’s entire business is invisible.
Fix: Film the origin story reel this week. It does not need to be polished. Phone camera, natural light, authentic delivery. Script: “I’m Wendy. I lost 95 pounds. Here’s what most people don’t know — I’ve kept it off for 25 years. Not with a diet. Here’s exactly what changed.” This one video, boosted as a paid ad, could generate months of qualified leads.
P3 — Watch Item
Instagram Handle Mismatch Reduces Discoverability
@wendyswaytohealth doesn’t match the name Wendy Goodine. Anyone who hears about Wendy from a referral and searches her name on Instagram will struggle to find her. This is a minor friction point today but becomes more material as paid traffic and content reach grow.
Fix: Add “Wendy Goodine” prominently to the Instagram bio — either in the name field or the first line of bio. Alternatively, if creating a new account, consider @wendygoodine as the primary handle. Low urgency — fix it but don’t let it block the content sprint.
OK — Validated Asset
Content Voice and Copy Quality Are Already Strong
The confirmed website copy demonstrates a clear, empathetic, high-quality content voice that most coaches take years to develop. Wendy can write — she just needs to write more, and in public. The foundation of a strong content brand is already in place. The transition from “website copy” to “consistent reel content” is the only gap.
Maintain: Keep the empathetic, anti-restriction, pro-permanence voice in all content. It is genuinely differentiated from the restriction-heavy language most nutrition coaches use.
