Section 04
Constraint Diagnosis
P1 — Critical
No Niche, No Avatar, No Offer — The Foundation Must Come First
Without a defined niche and avatar, there is no content to create, no offer to sell, and no ads to run. Every other action is premature until this is clear. This is not a weakness — it is simply the first step that cannot be skipped.
Fix: Complete the IFCA Avatar Worksheet this week. Answer: Who specifically do I help? What specific problem do I solve? What specific result do I deliver? Once answered, every other step follows naturally.
P1 — Critical
No Content, No Camera Presence, No Online Evidence of Expertise
Online coaching requires trust, and trust at scale requires a consistent on-camera presence. There is no shortcut. The willingness to get on camera and share knowledge consistently is the single non-negotiable requirement of building an online coaching brand from scratch.
Fix: Film one talking-head reel this week. It does not need to be polished. It needs to exist. Post it. Do it again the next week. The only path is through.
P2 — Gap
No First Clients Yet — Warm Network Outreach Needed Before Ads
Ads require proof, testimonials, and a tested offer to convert well. The fastest path to first clients is the warm network: friends, family, acquaintances who could benefit from the service or know someone who could. These first clients are not the business — they are the proof base that makes the business.
Fix: Message 20 people in your existing network this week. Not a pitch — a question: “I’m building an online coaching business focused on [niche]. Do you know anyone who might be interested in getting a free consultation?”
OK — Validated
Starting Point Doesn’t Determine Destination
Every successful IFCA client started at zero. The Phase 1 foundation— clear avatar, named mechanism, on-camera content, first clients from warm network — is the same playbook that builds $10K, $30K, and $100K/month businesses. The process is proven. The only variable is execution.
Fix: Trust the framework. Avatar clarity + first reel + first client inquiry is 100% achievable in 30 days.
Section 05
90-Day Action Plan
1
Week 1: Complete Avatar Worksheet — Pick Your Niche
Use the IFCA Avatar Worksheet. Decide: who specifically do I help, what specific problem, what specific result. Write it in one sentence: “I help [specific person] achieve [specific result] in [specific timeframe] without [specific objection].” Do not move to Step 2 until this is written.
Offer
2
Week 2: Name Your Mechanism + Write the Offer
Name the program. Name the method. Set the price ($1,500–$3,000 for a 12-week program). Write the offer document: what is included, what the client will achieve, what happens each week.
Offer
3
Weeks 3–4: Get On Camera — 3 Reels Per Week
Film 3 reels per week using the starter hook bank above. They do not need to be perfect. They need to demonstrate knowledge and personality. Every reel ends with a lead capture CTA.
Content
4
Month 2: Warm Network Outreach + First Client
Message 20+ people in your network. Goal: 1–3 clients from warm outreach by end of Month 1. These are your first testimonials, your first proof, and the validation that the offer works.
Sales
5
Month 3: First Paid Ad After Proof Exists
Once 1–2 clients have results, build a simple ad using the origin story hook or transformation result. Budget: $15–25/day. Do not run ads before testimonials exist.
