Section 04
Constraint Diagnosis
P1 · Critical Constraint
Pricing Architecture Compresses Margin to Zero
At $2,500 for 16 weeks including DUTCH labs ($300–500 hard cost), effective coaching revenue per client is $2,000–2,200 before any other overhead. At a standard 20% marketing margin target, that allows $400–440 in CAC — barely enough for organic referrals, nowhere near enough to run profitable paid ads at scale. This single pricing decision is the structural ceiling blocking the entire scaling path.
The Fix: Restructure to $3,500 core offer (clients pay labs directly out-of-pocket at their own lab provider). Add a VIP tier at $4,500+ that includes a co-pay for labs as a concierge upgrade. This immediately increases effective margin by 35–40% and makes $8–10 CPL ads instantly profitable. The offer value perception actually increases when labs are positioned as a premium diagnostic investment the client is making in themselves.
P1 · Critical Constraint
Zero Paid Traffic — 100% Organic Dependence
Every lead Loren generates today comes from organic content performance. One algorithm shift, one viral miss, or one bad month of content output means zero leads. Scaling from $10K to $25K on organic alone requires either a massive audience build (years) or unsustainable posting volume. The "Post & Pray" model is the P2 IFCA archetype — and the fix is a predictable paid lead engine feeding her existing DM automation funnel.
The Fix: Launch a single Meta campaign: 1 broad ad set + 1 interest-stacked ad set (hormone health, functional medicine, moms). Deploy 8–10 entities from the build queue above. Target $8 CPL or below. After pricing restructure, break-even CAC is ~$700–800 with a 30% close rate. $3K/month in ad spend should generate $25–30K in contracted revenue. Install the IFCA kill rules: new ads killed at 2x KPI, proven winners at 3x.
P2 · Meaningful Gap
No Standalone Mid-Funnel Conversion Asset for Cold Traffic
The DM automation keyword "BALANCE" is a functional conversion tool for warm organic audiences who have already seen Loren's content. For cold paid traffic, there is no standalone landing page, quiz, or lead magnet that can convert a stranger who has never heard of her. Cold traffic needs a low-friction first step — a quiz that diagnoses their hormone type, a free guide, or a specific opt-in with a compelling headline. Without this, paid ads will drive traffic with no place to land.
The Fix: Build a hormone quiz funnel — "Find out why you're always tired, even when you do everything right." 5-question quiz → result page → free guide download → DM automation CTA → assessment booking. Jordan's recommendation: model April Rodriguez's quiz structure. Estimated build time: 1–2 weeks on GoHighLevel or Typeform. This asset will also double the organic DM conversion rate by giving a cleaner handoff from viral content viewers.
P2 · Meaningful Gap
Back-End Offer Not Formalized — LTV Capped at Front-End Price
The Precision Rebuild back-end has conceptual existence but no formal structure, pricing, or client ascension path. When clients complete the 16-week Hormone Reset Club, they either churn out or continue informally. Without a formal $300–500/month Precision Rebuild continuity offer, Loren's LTV is capped at $2,500 (or $3,500 post-restructure). The IFCA model requires LTV of $6K–12K+ to make paid ads economically viable at scale.
The Fix: Formalize Precision Rebuild as a $400/month 6-month minimum commitment. Present it in the sales call as "Phase 1 gets you stable — Phase 2 gets you strong." Target 60%+ FE→BE conversion. 10 retained clients at $400/mo = $4K/month in passive recurring revenue added to front-end acquisition. This doubles effective LTV from $3,500 to $5,900 without acquiring a single new client.
OK · Validated Asset — Leave Alone
Organic Voice, Hook Quality, and Enemy Framing
Loren's content voice, emotional resonance with her avatar, and hook construction are genuinely above-average for this niche. The enemy framing ("diet culture makes exhaustion worse"), the identity reframes, and the empathy-driven educational content are all functioning correctly. This is not a message-market mismatch problem — it is a distribution and infrastructure problem. Do not change the messaging. Build the machine around it.
Maintain: Continue organic cadence at 3–5 Reels/week. Repurpose winning organic content directly as paid entities without significant modification. The hooks that already connect with warm audiences are the same hooks that will connect with cold paid audiences in the same demographic.
