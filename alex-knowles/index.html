Strategic Diagnosis

Alex’s situation is a classic credentialing mismatch — elite credentials priced like a commodity service. The fix sequence is: (1) Reprice immediately. (2) Consolidate to one domain. (3) Lead every piece of content with the SNBF Pro credential. (4) Build the AIR3 Executive Recomposition Protocol as the named offer. The existing organic hook (“Muscle Mass After 40”) is the first ad to run. His current clients are the first testimonials to capture. Both exist today — they just need to be deployed correctly.

Section 04
Constraint Diagnosis
P1 — Critical / Revenue Blocker
Catastrophic Underpricing — $30/Session for Pro-Level Credentials
$360 for 12 Zoom sessions equals $30/session effective rate. This is gym floor PT pricing applied to specialized, credential-backed online coaching for a premium demographic. Busy professionals over 40 with disposable income are not price-sensitive — they are quality-sensitive. Low pricing signals low value and actively repels the buyer Alex is trying to attract. An executive who spends $2,000/month on business coaching will not hire a $30/session fitness coach. The price itself is the conversion barrier.
Fix: Immediately restructure to the AIR3 Executive Recomposition Protocol: 4-month commitment, $2,000–$2,500 investment. When transitioning existing clients, offer a “founding member” rate of $1,500 for the first cohort. Remove the free 3-day trial entirely — replace with a free 20-minute Strategy Call as the lead-to-client conversion mechanism.
P1 — Critical / Brand Blocker
Fragmented Brand Across 3 Domains — Split SEO + Zero Brand Equity
Operating across air3.us, airthree.net, and alexknowlespt.com means every piece of content, every backlink, and every word-of-mouth referral points to a different property. SEO authority is split three ways, meaning none of the domains rank strongly. A professional over 40 searching for a coach online cannot find Alex because his digital presence is scattered across three low-authority properties.
Fix: Choose one primary domain this week (airthree.net recommended — cleanest). Set 301 redirects from the other two. Build a single professional landing page: AIR3 Executive Recomposition Protocol, the SNBF Pro credential front and center, one CTA (book a strategy call). The landing page should be ready to receive paid traffic within 30 days.
P2 — Meaningful Gap
Pro Bodybuilding Credential Invisible in Content Strategy
8 years as an SNBF Pro is the most differentiating credential in Alex’s arsenal and it is essentially absent from visible content. Generic motivational content dominates while the one thing that makes Alex genuinely different from the 50,000 other over-40 fitness coaches — proven competitive bodybuilding at the pro level — is buried or not shown at all. The credential needs to lead every hook.
Fix: Every content piece for the next 30 days opens with the credential: “As an 8-year pro bodybuilder…” Use stage competition photos in content. Build an origin story reel: “Here’s what 8 years of competitive bodybuilding taught me that changed how I coach my over-40 clients.”
P3 — Watch Item
Free 3-Day Trial Funnel Leaks High-Intent Leads
A free trial attracts tire-kickers and signals that the service may not be worth paying for upfront. High-ticket buyers ($2,000+) do not expect free trials — they expect a premium consultation experience. The free trial also means Alex is doing 3 days of work for leads who may never convert.
Fix: Replace with a 20-minute free Strategy Call. This filters for serious buyers, allows qualification, and begins the relationship at a premium level. Done alongside the repricing, this change alone will improve close rates significantly.
OK — Validated Asset
Age-Specific Hook Has Proven Organic Performance
The “What Happens to Muscle Mass After 40” hook is confirmed to perform organically. This is not a guess — this is a validated content concept ready to be built into a full entity cluster and used as paid ad creative. This single asset, properly deployed, can generate the first wave of HT leads without any new content creation.
Maintain + Scale: Build 4–5 more pieces in the “muscle after 40” education series. Boost the best-performing one as a Meta ad at $20–30/day. This is the ad to launch first.
